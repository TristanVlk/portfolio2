Meta Ads Case Study: Full-Funnel (Lead Gen + Sales)

Lead Gen Campaigns (Jun 3, 2024 – Aug 20, 2024)

Campaigns Monitored: 7 · Objective: Generate qualified leads & purchases while keeping CPC low.

  • Total Ad Spend: $8,850.92
  • Total Leads: 933
  • Link Clicks: 13,249
  • CPC: $0.67
  • CTR: 2.74%
  • Landing Page Views: 8,780
  • Avg. Cost / Purchase: $632.21
  • Avg. ROAS: 19.43x (Best: 51.95x)
  • Purchase Conversion Value: $171,973.03

Sales Campaigns (Mar 1, 2023 – Mar 31, 2023)

Objective: Drive website purchases and scale ROAS.

  • Total Ad Spend: $21,424.69
  • Reach: 450,416
  • Link Clicks: 18,797
  • Avg. CPC: $1.14
  • Total Purchases: 1,018
  • Cost / Purchase: $21.04
  • ROAS (Avg): 2.41x
  • Purchase Conversion Value: $51,174.47

Summary: Executed a full-funnel strategy — top-of-funnel lead gen to capture interest at low CPC and bottom-of-funnel purchase campaigns to convert users. Combined, the accounts generated hundreds of leads and over 1,000 purchases across campaigns while producing strong returns and measurable revenue impact.
